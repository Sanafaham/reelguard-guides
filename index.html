Why comments are where sales get lost

Most Instagram sellers focus on creating content. The reel goes up. The views come in. And then buyers show up in the comments asking real questions. Price, availability, shipping, how to order. They type their question and wait.

If no reply comes within an hour or two, most of them are gone. Not because they did not want to buy. Because they moved on.

This is not a content problem. Your content worked. The comment happened because your content worked. The sale was lost after that, in a place most sellers are not watching closely enough.
